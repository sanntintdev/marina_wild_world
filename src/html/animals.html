Discover Our Wildlife

From majestic big cats to playful penguins, meet our incredible family of animals. Each species has a unique story of survival and adaptation.

Endangered

African Lions

Meet our pride of African lions, including our majestic male Zeus and his family. These apex predators are a sight to behold during feeding times.

Vulnerable

Asian Elephants

Our elephant sanctuary houses a family of gentle giants.

Endangered

Red Panda

Watch the adorable Red Panda in action! With its fluffy tail and mischievous eyes, this small forest dweller is a favorite for kids and adults alike.

Endangered

Sumatran Tigers

Our magnificent tigers demonstrate their natural behaviors in a spacious habitat.

Endangered

Bornean Orangutan

Meet these playful, curious apes who swing through the trees! Get inspired by their survival story and discover what you can do to help preserve their jungle homes.

Valurable

Malayan Tapir

With its distinct black and white coloring, the Malayan Tapir is a fascinating forest dweller. Catch a glimpse of this unique animal and learn how it helps keep rainforests thriving.

Valurable

Komodo Dragon

Meet the ultimate island predator! Our Komodo Dragon is a living relic of the prehistoric world, and here’s your chance to see this powerful creature up close.

Endangered

White Rhinoceros

Behold the strength of the White Rhino, a true giant of the African plains. Discover the inspiring story of its survival amidst threats in the wild.

Protected

Proboscis Monkey

Come laugh with the quirky, long-nosed Proboscis Monkey! Native to Borneo, this fascinating primate is known for its playful nature and amusing expressions.

Endangered

Sumatran Orangutan

Meet one of the last wild men of the jungle! This intelligent and gentle great ape is battling habitat loss, and you can see its amazing abilities up close in our Rainforest Habitat.

Protected

Emperor Penguins

Watch our colony of penguins in their climate-controlled habitat.
